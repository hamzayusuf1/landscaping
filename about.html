Welcome to GreenScape, your trusted family-owned landscaping company based in the heart of Birmingham. We are passionate about transforming outdoor spaces into breathtaking havens, and we've been doing just that for families and businesses in our community for over 20 years.

Our journey began with a simple love for nature and a desire to enhance the beauty of our surroundings. We have always had deep roots in Birmingham, and our commitment to creating stunning landscapes has been passed down through generations. What started as a small, local endeavour has grown into a thriving landscaping business, all while maintaining the core values of integrity, quality, and exceptional customer service.
